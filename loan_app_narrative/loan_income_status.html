Behind every loan approval, there stands great information about a client

Income Vs Loan Amount across loan portfolio

Please click the legend status symbol to see each portfolio and distribution of income among them. Hover over the points to see more details of applicant

In this scatterplot we are showing the distribution of income and loan amount and if it all they are correlated. We see most the approved loans of higher amount does belong to people having higher income. We do have couple of outliers thought and although this is not a very balanced dataset , its still worth mentioning that borrowers income and the loan amount are related and play significant role in loan approval/rejection.

Also interesting thing to look at most of approved loans of high income borrower seems to be male and not self employed.
